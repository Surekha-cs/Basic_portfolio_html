Advanced Price Optimization System
Project Overview

Developed an AI-driven price optimization platform that dynamically recommends optimal product prices to maximize revenue, profit margins, and market competitiveness. The solution integrates historical sales data, customer behavior, competitor pricing, inventory levels, and demand forecasting to support data-driven pricing decisions.


Key Features:

1. Demand Forecasting :

    Built machine learning models to predict product demand.
    Incorporated seasonality, promotions, holidays, and market trends.
    Improved forecast accuracy using ensemble learning techniques.

2. Price Elasticity Modeling:

Analyzed customer sensitivity to price changes.
Estimated elasticity coefficients for different product categories.
Identified optimal price ranges for maximizing profit.

3. Competitor Price Intelligence

Collected competitor pricing data through APIs and web scraping.
Monitored market positioning and price gaps.
Generated competitive pricing recommendations.

4. Dynamic Pricing Engine
Applied optimization algorithms to determine optimal prices.
Considered business constraints such as:
Minimum margin thresholds
Inventory availability
Market demand
Competitor prices

5. Scenario Analysis Dashboard
Enabled business users to simulate pricing scenarios.
